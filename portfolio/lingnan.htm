Design features

Post-it Notes

Post-it notes allow students to move their ideas around on paper quickly. It's a fun way to learn and sequence their ideas. Adding the Post-it notes as a Call-to-action design element on the website helps to attract students attention and increases their connection to the program.

Academic Blue

We use academic blue as a subtitle heading background throughout the entire website. This academic blue is a mixture of cyan blue, often giving audiences the feeling of wisdom, confidence and intelligence. It is a great colour choice to match the academic theme.

Serious Learning, Serious Fun

In 2019, Prof. KUEHNER Stefan from Lingnan University, Department of Sociology and Social Policy, offered to redesign the website of MSocSci in Comparative Social Policy (International). Stefan mentioned that the topic, Comparative Social Policy, might sound a bit serious, but he favoured to the interactive teaching styles for engaging their students. He would like me, as a designer, to redesign the program page with a good balance of fun and academic elements to match his teaching philosophy.

This project was completed in 6 weeks, from research, wireframes draft, presentation to launch. There were total 7 pages, design available online from 2019-2021.
