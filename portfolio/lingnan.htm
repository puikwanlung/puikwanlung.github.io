Serious Learning • Serious Fun

Post-it notes allow students to move their ideas around on paper quickly. It's a fun way to learn and sequence their ideas. Adding the Post-it notes as a “Call-to-action” design element on the website helps to attract students attention and increases their connection to the program.

The ribbon title also helps to softens the program seriousness.

Academic Blue

This academic blue is a mixture of cyan blue, often giving audiences the feeling of wisdom, confidence and intelligence. It is a great colour choice to match the academic theme.
