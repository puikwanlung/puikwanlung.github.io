Living Space

Initial thoughts

Create an apartment rental app for landlords and talents, mainly targeting international students who are new to the city. The app creates a one-stop platform to host or rent an apartment. Providing comprehensive range of services such as the deposit payment gateway and live chat with landlords allow people to rent their new home efficiently.
