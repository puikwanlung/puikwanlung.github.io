Design ideas

BTS, the seven-member South Korean boy band, released their second English single, 'Butter,'' in May 2021. The members showcase a new look and style for the release of Butter, including new hair colours, makeup and selected accessories.

We selected six members for this team project, including Jimin, Jin, RM, V, J-Hope and Jungkook. Each icon design is based on their personality, new looks and style, showcased in their newly released album Butter.
