Brand Guidelines

Sx3

Sx3 is a snowboard apparel brand. This brand guidelines is an inspirational tool for partners to effectively plan and execute product merchandising and brand communications.

Sx3 brand ideas

S x 3 means S times 3, each S represents one of our brand features - Safe, Strength and Spirit.

Safety is our priority. We want people to protect themselves on the slope while wearing the proper apparel. People can also gain strength and have a great sense of "Snowspirit" with our unique design of snowboard apparel.

The mountain in the logo connects the word S and the number 3. Mountain is an excellent symbol representing our brand, Sx3. People can easily connect the brand to nature-related products by simply seeing the logo. Mountains also symbolize overcoming obstacles, spiritual and improvement, matching our brand values.
