Pui Kwan Lung,
Interactive Media Designer

I believe that great design actually improves the quality of life, beyond the purely visual pleasure. As designers, we observe and analyze human behaviour and keep challenging ourselves to develop ideas that help solve problems and create a better experience. I live for the moment when we embrace and improvise ideas that spring from the imaginary world. Being able to manifest the ideas or concepts in the real world through imagery has been a fundamentally meaningful aspect of my happiness and sense of achievement. For me, design is about solving problems beautifully, changing the current situation into a better one; possibly even a bring a transcendence to the world. :)
